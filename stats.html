How to Use Text Stats

    Step 1: Paste Your Text

  • Paste the text you want to analyze into the text box.
  • Make sure to remove any unnecessary characters or formatting.

    • Step 2: Configure Your Analysis

  • Select the type of analysis you want to perform (e.g. word frequency, sentiment analysis, etc.).
  • Choose any additional settings or options as desired.

    • Step 3: Analyze Your Text

  • Press the "Analyze" button to run the analysis.
  • Wait for the results to be generated.

    • Step 4: View Your Results

  • Review the analysis results, which will be displayed below the text box.
  • Explore the various statistics and insights generated by Text Stats.
